About EcoImpact

Our Mission

Our goal is to create the most information rich, user friendly and interactive app to help designers, planners and constituents visualize scenarios of future development and better understand the impacts of our current environs. 

We are looking to create an immersive, 3D virtual environment that is easily navigatable, scalable, and editable and which provides visual layers of information regarding the environmental, economic and social impacts.

 

EcoImpact Purpose

As a first of its kind platform, the EcoImpact web app would potentially change the way our municipal leaders and building industry professionals make decisions about where and how to invest in our communities. It also has the potential potential to be an enormously powerful educational tool for uses as broad as students learning about the environment to a research tool on how cities metabolize resources in order to work toward attaining a better equilibrium.
